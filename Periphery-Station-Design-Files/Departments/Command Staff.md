#Departments

# Synopsis:
The command staff are responsible for both the general operations of the site, especially in the case of the Site Director, and assisting their specific departments. The Command Staff are all expected to act far closer to the laws and [[Command Staff#Standard Operating Procedures|Command SOP]], due to the significant [[Command Staff#Command Responsibilities|responsibility]] they all hold over the site's operation. Therefore, they must not only follow their own SOP, their department's, but also Command specific SOP.

# List of Roles:
- Site Director (Level 4-(Site Command), All Accesses)
- Ethics Committee Liaison (Level 4-(Ethics Command), All Accesses)

- Security Operations Director (Level 4-(Command), Containment Lab Accesses + Special Armoury Access + All Security Accesses)
- Engineering Operations Director (Level 4-(Command), Exterior Access + All Engineering Accesses + Containment Lab Accesses + Network Access)
- Research Operations Director (Level 4-(Command), All Lab Accesses + Containment Lab Accesses)
- .



# Command Responsibilities: 

- [[Redaction]]
	- Command staff are responsible for the Redaction of information that falls within the access restrictions beyond the staff that discovers it. Mostly a responsibility for the research department, but all department Directors are responsible for ensuring restricted information does not get into the wrong hands.
- [[Amnesticisation]]
	- Command staff are expected, along with the security department (and in rare cases, the research department), to amnesticise any personnel witnessing anything beyond their access, or anything that is better off forgotten.
